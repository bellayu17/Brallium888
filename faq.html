What makes Brallium's nipple covers different from traditional ones?

We go beyond basic coverage. Here's what sets them apart:

- Innovative Biomaterial: Made from eco-friendly biomaterials, we designed to minimize environmental impact for a truly sustainable choice.

- Inclusive Comfort: Our products cater to all skin types, including sensitive skin, providing a secure fit that reduces irritation and redness.

- Multifunctional: Two benefits in one, giving you more value with every wear-doubles as solid perfume and protection.

How long can I wear Brallium nipple covers?

Our nipple covers are built for durability and comfort, typically lasting up to 10 uses. They adhere securely and remove easily without leaving residue, allowing for a comfortable, all-day wear experience.

+

Are Brallium nipple covers reusable?

Yes! Thanks to our high-quality biomaterial, Brallium nipple covers can be easily cleaned after each use for multiple wears. This not only supports sustainable practices but also allows you to enjoy the comfort and convenience of reusability.

How do I take care of my nipple covers?

To keep your nipple covers in top condition, follow these simple steps:

1. Gently Clean: Hand-wash with mild soap, avoiding harsh chemicals or large amount of running water.

2. Store Properly: Air dried and place back in the original protective case to maintain their shape and adhesive quality.
